PROJECT DESCRIPTION 

The Digital Literacy Project Assignment included the creation of this repository. The project aims to assist peers in comprehending and successfully navigating the digital world by simulating the role of a student digital ambassador.

The portfolio addresses key facets of digital literacy, such as:

-Knowledge of the internet

-Expert internet presence

-Tools for online collaboration

-Email protocol

-Awareness of cybercrime

As needed, all tasks are arranged into structured folders.

PROJECT STRUCTURE:

Task 1: Creating an Infographic on Digital Literacy
Develop an infographic on the topic of digital literacy, including internet safety, professional online presence, and the necessary digital tools for students, using the canva.

Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
Developed professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and [Other Platform], created a GitHub user profile README, and added education information to create a strong and credible student presence online

Task 3: Platforms Exploration
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on [Platform Name] and created a Digital Literacy Quiz using Google Forms, improving not only problem-solving skills but also awareness of collaboration tools.

Task 4: Email Etiquette
Wrote two professional email examples for academic and internship purposes, and also came up with a comprehensive checklist of what students should do and avoid doing under the topic of Social Media Do’s and Don’ts.

Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Developed a case study on a chosen topic of cybercrime, discussing the procedure of the crime, the risks involved, and the impact of the crime, along with a checklist for preventing the crime and awareness of how to report it through the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline number 1930.
